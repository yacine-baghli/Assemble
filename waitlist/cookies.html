Cookies and storage

No advertising trackers.

Last updated: 11 July 2026

The current Assemble website does not use advertising, retargeting, or cross-site analytics cookies.

1. Theme preference

The site stores assemble-theme in your browser's local storage when you choose light or dark mode. This preference is used only to remember the interface you requested. It is not used to identify you or track activity across websites.

2. Tally form

The early access form is embedded from Tally. Loading or using it may cause Tally to process essential technical information needed to display, secure, and submit the form. Tally states that its website uses essential cookies. Review Tally's cookie notice for current information.

3. Consent banner

Because the current site intentionally avoids non-essential trackers, it does not display a consent banner. If analytics, advertising, social-media, or other non-essential trackers are added later, they must remain blocked until the visitor makes a valid choice. Refusing must be as easy as accepting, and settings must remain accessible.

4. Browser controls

You can clear local storage and cookies through your browser settings. Clearing the theme preference returns the website to your operating system's current light or dark preference.

5. Contact

Questions about cookies or storage may be sent to [privacy email].
