# Assemble Website

## Vision

A production-oriented early access website for Assemble, an AI talent search and outreach agent that can find a cofounder, an employee, a specialist, or an entire team and carry the workflow through qualification and meeting handoff.

## Audience

Founders, operators, and teams that know what they want to build but need the right people to build it with.

## Core message

Find the right person. Or the whole team.
