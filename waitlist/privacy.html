2. Data we collect

  • Your email address.
  • The date and source of your registration and the consent information associated with it.
  • Technical information needed to secure and operate the form, such as basic request and error logs processed by Tally.

Providing your email is optional. Without it, we cannot send Assemble product updates or an early access invitation.

3. Why we use it

We use the data only to manage the Assemble early access list and send product news, launch information, invitations, and related marketing about the Assemble SaaS.

The legal basis is your consent under Article 6(1)(a) GDPR and the applicable electronic marketing rules. You may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.

4. Who receives it

Access is limited to authorized Assemble team members and processors needed to operate the waitlist and communications.

  • Tally BV, Belgium, provides the form service and acts as processor for form responses. Tally states that form data is encrypted and stored in Europe.
  • [email delivery provider, if used].

We do not sell your email address or share it with third parties for their independent marketing.

5. International transfers

Tally states that form data is stored in Europe. Some service providers may process limited technical data outside the European Economic Area under an applicable transfer mechanism. See Tally's GDPR information and its subprocessor list for current details.

6. Retention

Prospect data used for marketing is kept for up to three years from collection or the latest active contact from you, unless you withdraw consent sooner. Data needed to document an opt-out may be retained for up to three years so that we can respect it.

7. Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your data. You may withdraw consent and object to marketing at any time using the unsubscribe link in each email or by contacting [privacy email].

If you believe your rights have not been respected after contacting us, you may submit a complaint to the CNIL or your local supervisory authority.

8. Security and changes

We use reasonable organizational and technical measures to protect waitlist data. We may update this notice when the service, providers, or legal requirements change. The current version and effective date will remain available on this page.
